About MusicWorld

Founded in 2023, The aim is to grow into one of the largest online Artist tellling website on the web. Our expertise in all things tech has resulted in millions of visitors every month and hundreds of thousands of fans on social media. We believe that technology is only as useful as the one who uses it. Our aim is to tell the readers like you about the top latest artist of the week and the global hits, explained in simple terms that anyone can understand.
